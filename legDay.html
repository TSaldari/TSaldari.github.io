Leg Day


Time to build that foundation!

Quads

Description: The quadricep femoris (quads) muscle is one of the largest muscle in the human body. It is located on the front of the leg above the knee. Its main functionality is to extend the knee.

Muscle parts: The quads have four sub muscles, these include the: Rectus frmoris (Blue), Vastus lateralis (Yellow), Vastus medialis (Green), and the Vastus intermedius (Under the Recuts femoris).

Source: Physiopedia



Source: BodyBuilding.com

Workouts By Level


Level Exercise Benefits Video
Beginner Workouts Seated Leg Extension Easy, extremely effective, great isolation, and safe.
Intermediate Workouts Barbell Back Squat Extremely popular, one of the big 3 compound lifts, whole lower body movement with a focus on the quads.
Advanced Workouts Bulgarian Split Squat Separate quad development, great isolation, great range of motion.

Hamstrings

Description: The hamstrings muscle is a large msucle located on the back of the leg above the knee. Its main functionality is to flex the knee.

Muscle parts: The hamstrings have three sub muscles, these include the: Semimembranosus (Blue), Biceps femoris (Yellow), and Semitendinosus (Green).

Source: Physiopedia



Source: BodyBuilding.com

Workouts By Level


Level Exercise Benefits Video
Beginner Workouts Seated Leg Curl Easy, extremely effective, great isolation, and safe.
Intermediate Workouts Romanian Deadlift (RDL) Effective, improves hip hinge mechanics, compound movement targeting the hamstrings.
Advanced Workouts Nordic Hamstring Curl Increases knee stability, reduces injury risk, and increases explosiveness.

Glutes

Description: The gluteus maximus (glutes) muscle is the largest muscle in the human body. It is located on the back of the hip joint. Its primary function is to maintain posture and hip extensions (main power generator for running and lifting).

Muscle parts: The glutes is a single muscle, and has no subparts.


Source: Physiopedia

Workouts By Level


Level Exercise Benefits Video
Beginner Workouts Standing Glute Kickback Easy and safe movement.
Intermediate Workouts Barbell Hip Thrust Effective, accessible with heavy loads, and helps pelvic stability.
Advanced Workouts Standing Barbell Goodmorning Improves hip mobility, reduces risk of injury, and strengthens the glutes, lower back, and hamstrings.

Calves

Description: The calf muscle is a long muscle on the back of the leg that stretches from the knee joint to the heel. Its primary function is to point toes downwards and raise the heel.

Muscle parts: The calf has three muscle, these include the: Gastronemius, Soleus, and Plantaris.


Source: Physiopedia

Workouts By Level


Level Exercise Benefits Video
Beginner Workouts Seated Calf Raise Easy, effective, large range of motion, good isolation, safe, and targets soleus primarily.
Intermediate Workouts Standing Machine Calf Raise Easy, effective, large range of motion, good isolation, and targets gastrocnemius primarily.
Advanced Workouts Barbell Overhead Press Targets both the soleus and gastrocnemius, easy, can be used in a superset with leg press (Another great quad/hamstring compound movement).
